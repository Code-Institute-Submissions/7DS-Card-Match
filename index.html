Seven Deadly Sins Card Match

Flips

0

Instructions

Cards start facing down. On the other side of each card there is an image. There are two of each images that have been mixed up throughout the board. Match the two images by turning one card over at a time. If the two cards flipped do not match, then they flip back over. Try to remember where the mathcing pairs are. For a challenge try to complete in as little flips as possible.
